IT TCO Calculator
Free calculator to compare the Total Cost of Ownership of IT equipment over 1 to 7 years.

It helps compare hardware beyond purchase price by including:

purchase cost

electricity consumption

annual maintenance

estimated failure cost

residual resale value

Why this project is useful
Many buyers compare hardware only on upfront price. In practice, a device with a higher purchase price can become cheaper over time thanks to lower power draw, lower maintenance, and better residual value.

This calculator makes that reasoning explicit and shareable — export your comparison as a summary or share the URL.
